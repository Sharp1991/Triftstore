About Us

Urban Wardrobe

Who We Are

Urban Wardrobe is a homegrown company from Meghalaya, Northeast India, built with the vision of bringing affordable and stylish fashion to everyone. We focus on sustainability, and customer trust, ensuring each piece is carefully selected and maintained.

From trendy casuals to timeless classics, our collection blends comfort, quality, and individuality. Whether you are shopping for daily wear or special occasions, we provide products that let you express yourself without spending too much.

Meet Our Founder

Jenny Michelle Wankhar

Jenny Michelle Wankhar is the visionary founder of Urban Fashion. Passionate about sustainable fashion, she aims to empower local communities while offering affordable, stylish clothing. Her dedication to quality and customer trust forms the backbone of Urban Wardrobe success.
