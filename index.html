About Thrift Sixteen

Who We Are

Thrift Sixteen is a homegrown company from Meghalaya, Northeast India, built with the vision of bringing affordable and stylish thrift fashion to everyone. We focus on hygiene, sustainability, and customer trust, ensuring each piece is carefully selected and maintained.

From trendy casuals to timeless classics, our collection blends comfort, quality, and individuality. Whether you are shopping for daily wear or special occasions, we provide products that let you express yourself without spending too much.
